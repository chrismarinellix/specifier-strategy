01 — The Challenge

The Specifier Gap

While your direct-to-consumer and builder channels perform well, a critical segment remains unreached—the architects and interior designers who specify materials for high-value commercial and residential projects.

Fragmented Market

Thousands of architecture firms across Australia make traditional direct outreach resource-intensive and inefficient.

Relationship-Driven

Architects are loyal to responsiveness and expertise, not advertising. They seek consultative partners who understand their workflow.

Digital Discovery

59% discover brands via search engines. 54% research manufacturer websites. Your digital presence must serve their specification needs.

"Architects are by and large the most important influencers in the specification process. Focus less on selling—focus more on building relationships."

— Industry Research, 2025

Twelve Months to Partnership

Three strategic phases designed to build lasting relationships with Australia's specifier community.

Foundation

  • Build specifier portal with BIM/CAD downloads
  • Create comprehensive technical specifications
  • Develop architect sample box program
  • Identify top 100 target architecture firms

  • Launch AIA-accredited CPD program
  • Register for Design Show Australia
  • Partner with architecture publications
  • Create project gallery with installations
Growth

  • Execute Design Show Australia presence
  • Begin lunch & learn program (15 firms)
