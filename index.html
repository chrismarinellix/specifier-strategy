Specifier Strategy

A 12-month strategic plan to penetrate the architect and interior designer market for premium bathware specification

January 2026

The Challenge

Core Problem: Inability to effectively penetrate the specifier market (architects and interior designers), limiting access to high-value commercial and residential projects.

While successful in direct-to-consumer and builder sales, the company lacks a systematic approach to engage the influential specifier segment that dictates purchasing decisions on major projects.

The large number of architecture and design firms makes traditional direct outreach resource-intensive and inefficient. A strategic, scalable approach is required.

Key Risks

  • Missing high-value commercial projects
  • Limited brand prestige in premium market
  • Competitors gaining specifier loyalty
  • Market share growth ceiling
  • Reduced pricing power

The Strategic Approach

A 12-month program structured in three phases: Foundation, Growth, and Scale. Focus on relationship building, not selling.

01

Foundation

Months 1-4: Build digital infrastructure, create specifier resources, establish CPD program, and identify target firms.

02

Growth

Months 5-8: Launch relationship programs, attend key trade shows, deliver CPD sessions, build firm library presence.

03

Scale

Months 9-12: Expand successful channels, leverage case studies, pursue industry awards, optimize based on data.
